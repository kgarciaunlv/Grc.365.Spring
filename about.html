About Stingrays

A common misconception about stingrays is that they are dangerous animals that actively attack humans. In reality, stingrays are very shy creatures that usually hide under sand and only swim away when approached.

Another misconception is that their barb is used for hunting. In truth, it is only a defense tool used when they feel threatened. Most stingray injuries happen accidentally when people step on them in shallow water.
