Many stories describe her living on the mountains, especially near K朝lauea. Hawaiians respect Pele because she represents both destruction and creation.

Hawaii is home to some of the most dangerous volcanoes in the world. K朝lauea is one of the most active volcanoes on Earth and erupts often, sending lava flows toward communities. Mauna Loa, the largest volcano in the world, is also very dangerous because of its size and ability to produce fast-moving lava.
