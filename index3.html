The Island's Deeper Dive

Hawaii is home to many unique animals that live nowhere else in the world. Because the islands are far from other land, many animals evolved in special ways to survive there. Some native animals include the Hawaiian monk seal and the nēnē, which is Hawaii’s state bird. However, not all animals in Hawaii are native. Some were brought by humans, which changed the environment in big ways.
